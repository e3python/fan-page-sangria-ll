About 'Showdown!'

Pokemon Showdown is a fan-made battle simulator webpage that allows players to engage in Pokemon battles online without the need to purchase any game.

Features

  • Use randomized teams or curate your own in the teambuilder
  • Play with random opponents or challenge friends
  • Access to several competitive formats, both official and non-official
  • Customizable rulesets with unique attributes
